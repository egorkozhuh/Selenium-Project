Information about the service

Instant top-up of a mobile phone account

Ran out of money on the account? No problem! PrivatBank makes it easy for you to top up your mobile phone account wherever you are. The main condition is the presence of a mobile phone, Internet, payment card.

Convenient services, unlimited possibilities!

Ways to instantly increase balance:
  • ATM;
  • Privat24 service;
  • self-service terminal;
  • by number «3700»;
  • online through SMS-banking.

Website for topping up - minimum commission and maximum reliability!

Even if you went on a tour to the middle of nowhere, you can replenish the account on the phone using the SMS Banking and Privat24 applications. You can be anywhere, we work everywhere! Online service - the most instant and popular way!

You decide when to top up your phone! Now you do not depend on the cashier or bank branches! What is nice: the commission is symbolic, it does not depend on the amount, if it is not conducted from credit funds!

Site and the mobile application «Privat24»

Everything is very simple! After registration, go to the application or to the web-page of online banking "Privat24". Perform the operation on the main page or in the “All Services” rubricator, the “Communication Services” paragraph. Next, choose one method out of three. A very convenient option “via the Internet”, which is represented by the account top up site - “Mass replenishment”: create a list, press the button - the balance is increased for a group of users! The “Archive” option allows you to save time by using ready-made templates of “familiar” payments. The template will be saved after you perform the action just once.

How to top up?

The user enters the number into the ready-made form (whether it is foreign or Ukrainian). Then the amount and the debit or credit card of “PrivatBank” (or other account) are indicated. After confirming the payment with a one-time SMS password, the money will be credited to the mobile balance within a minute. If you send money to your own mobile number, then confirmation is not required.

Top up your phone account by using SMS-banking

The SMS Banking application has been developed for iOS and Android. The one touch solution allows you to top up your mobile balance. When the website or the Privat24 application is unavailable, for example, due to the absence of the Internet, SMS Banking will allow you to always stay connected. International top up of the account is also carried out promptly, regardless of the country and operator.
